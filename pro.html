What Pro will be

Scoped honestly, announced when real.

Pro is a planned edition. The list below separates what already exists in the shared CMYKForge foundation from what is planned — nothing here is a commitment.

The foundation is already being built.

Everything in CMYKForge Standard — image analysis, CMYK/CMYKW planning, model generation, labeled previews, printability checks, and 3MF/STL/OBJ export — is the base Pro inherits. Standard is in active development today.

Optional intelligence, on your machine.

Pro is planned to add optional local AI assistance for color guidance and workflow suggestions — running locally, never required, never part of Standard. Scope, naming, and timing may change.

"Claims follow physical prints. Pro will be announced feature by feature, as each one becomes real."

Local first

Built on a workflow that stays yours.

The CMYKForge core is designed around local use — your images are processed on your machine, without cloud processing. Which Pro-specific features run fully offline will be documented as the edition takes shape.

Requirements

What you'll need.

Pro-specific requirements have not been published. As an edition built on the CMYKForge foundation, expect at least the published Standard requirements — printer, filament, macOS, and Windows details are maintained in one place on the Standard page.
