About CMYKForge

Building a better workflow for full-spectrum color FDM printing.

CMYKForge is being built around a simple belief: color 3D printing should be easier to understand, easier to preview, and easier to improve.

Today, image-based color FDM printing often depends on trial and error. Loaded filament colors, slicer behavior, material translucency, layer thickness, printer calibration, and export settings can all change the final result. CMYKForge is being developed to bring those decisions into one clearer workflow.

The goal is to help creators move from a flat image to a color-aware 3D printable model using CMYK-style material planning, filament profiles, model generation, preview tools, printability checks, and slicer-compatible export.

CMYKForge Standard is the first step in that vision: a local-first, non-AI version focused on the core workflow. Future versions may add optional AI assistance, deeper calibration tools, and professional workflow features, but the foundation remains the same — give creators more control over color before the print begins.

Mission

Our Mission

CMYKForge's mission is to make full-spectrum color FDM printing more predictable, more practical, and more accessible. The platform is being designed to help creators prepare image-based prints with better color planning, clearer previews, and stronger export workflows.

Motivation

Why CMYKForge Exists

Color FDM printing has huge potential, but the workflow can still feel disconnected. Images, filament choices, slicer settings, and printed results often do not line up cleanly. CMYKForge is being built to connect those steps into a more intentional process.

Less Guesswork

CMYKForge is designed to help users plan color before exporting instead of relying only on trial-and-error testing.

More Control

The workflow focuses on image analysis, CMYK-style material planning, filament profiles, and preview tools.

Real Print Workflows

CMYKForge is designed to support slicer-compatible exports and practical multi-material FDM printing.

Vision

The Vision

CMYKForge is not just a file generator. It is being built as a color-focused workflow for creators who want to push multi-material FDM printing further. The long-term vision includes calibration systems, filament profile management, advanced preview simulation, slicer interoperability, optional AI guidance, and tools for both beginner and professional users.

AI stance

AI Is Optional

CMYKForge is not built around requiring AI. CMYKForge Standard is the local-first, non-AI version of the product. Future AI tools may be offered separately to assist with calibration, color guidance, printability, and workflow recommendations, but the core CMYKForge workflow is designed to stand on its own.

AI is never required for the core CMYKForge workflow.
Development

Still in Development

CMYKForge is currently in development. Features, workflows, export behavior, compatibility, and availability may change as the platform improves. Early beta feedback will help shape the product before wider release.

CMYKForge began as an attempt to solve a real problem in color 3D printing: turning images into printable color models without depending entirely on guesswork. The project is being built with a focus on print quality, color accuracy, reliability, and practical workflows for real multi-material printers.
