Early preview

A long way out.

CMYKForge Local AI is a planned future edition. It is not in development yet, has no release window, and every detail on this page may change. CMYKForge Standard — the non-AI core workflow — is the current focus.

This page is an early preview of intent, not a product announcement. Features, naming, pricing, and timing have not been finalized.
What it aims to add

Optional assistance, on your machine

Building on the Standard workflow. These are current intentions from the roadmap — not committed features.

Color Guidance

Aims to offer optional local AI assistance for color decisions within the CMYKForge workflow.

Workflow Suggestions

Aims to suggest workflow improvements while leaving every decision in your hands.

Local by Design

Planned to run on your machine. Cloud AI is a separate future concept — and never part of Standard.

Built on Standard

Standard stays complete without it

CMYKForge Standard is the non-AI foundation and remains fully usable on its own. Local AI is planned as an optional layer on top — AI is never required for the core color workflow. See where it sits on the AI roadmap.
