What it is

A New Way to Generate Color in 3D Printing

CMYKForge is a color-focused model generation platform for multi-material FDM printing. Instead of matching an image only to the filament colors loaded in a printer, CMYKForge uses CMYK-style material planning, filament profiles, preview tools, and export systems to help prepare image-based prints with more control.

The goal is to reduce guesswork and make color preparation part of the model generation process from the beginning.

Image-Based Printing

Turn photos, artwork, logos, and designs into color-aware printable models.

CMYK / CMYKW Workflows

Prepare models around cyan, magenta, yellow, black, and optional white material strategies.

Slicer-Compatible Output

Generate model files intended for real slicer workflows.

A limited set of filaments becomes a full spectrum of color — planned, previewed, and prepared before the first layer prints.

The problem

Color FDM Printing Still Has Too Much Guesswork

Many image-based color printing workflows depend heavily on trial and error. Loaded filament colors, slicer behavior, material translucency, layer thickness, and calibration can all change the final result.

CMYKForge gives creators a clearer workflow before printing starts.

Limited Filament Colors

Most printers can only load a small number of materials at once.

Unpredictable Results

The preview, exported model, slicer result, and real print can look different.

Manual Tuning

Creators often have to adjust settings through repeated tests.

From Image Color to Printable Material Strategy

Color separation
01 — Channels

Start with separated color.

CMYKForge reads your image and splits it into cyan, magenta, yellow, and key channels — the raw ingredients of a wider printable color range.

02 — Alignment

Matched to your filaments.

Each channel is planned around the real materials you’ve loaded, using their transmission distance and hex values.

03 — Print

One color-aware result.

The channels converge into a single color-aware model, ready for multi-material FDM printing.

AI is optional

AI Is Optional

CMYKForge Standard delivers the core local workflow without requiring local or cloud AI. Future editions may add optional AI assistance, but image preparation, color planning, model generation, previewing, and export remain part of the independent CMYKForge foundation.

Standard Stays Non-AI
