Dev Log

Development Progress

Real updates on how CMYKForge is coming together. New entries are added as the work happens — subscribe to the newsletter to get them by email.

  1. Jul 2026
    Milestone

    Kickstarter campaign launched

    The CMYKForge Kickstarter campaign is live — a new way to 3D print images. Backing the campaign supports development of the local-first color printing workflow.

  2. Jul 2026
    Milestone

    First physical color-wheel print

    Added the first real physical print to the site's evidence section, shown alongside the original image and the CMYKForge preview. The preview stays clearly labelled as a simulation, and printed color depends on calibration and filament behavior.

  3. Jul 2026
    Milestone

    Newsletter is live

    The CMYKForge newsletter is open. You can now subscribe to follow development, beta information, print tests, and feature announcements — with double opt-in confirmation.

  4. Jul 2026
    Progress

    Website live at cmykforge.org

    The CMYKForge site is online — home, the Standard product page, shop, and about — with the interactive CMYK color-separation demo and the workflow walkthroughs.

  5. Ongoing
    In progress

    CMYKForge Standard in active beta development

    Work continues on the local-first workflow: image analysis, CMYK / CMYKW material planning, model generation, preview, printability checks, and slicer-compatible export. Tested with real printers, slicers, filaments, and prints.
